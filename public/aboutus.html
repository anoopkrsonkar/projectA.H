About Us

AreaHop is more than just a directory. The idea took shape when we were frustrated with finding multiple platforms to discover local businesses. So, we decided to build one.

We at AreaHop, offer every one of our users, fast and easy access to information about available services in their area. Whether it's sniffing the next great meal or hunting down the perfect hairdresser, we aim to take the complexity out of finding local venues. 

Vision

Uncovering places, businesses or Landmarks should be our pain and not yours. We aim to become a comprehensive local business discovery platform and offer an engaging content powered by you, for you.
